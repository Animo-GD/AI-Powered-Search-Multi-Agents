Executive Summary

The procurement analysis focused on NVIDIA GTX 1660 SUPER graphics cards sourced from Sigma Computer. Four key models were evaluated based on price, discount, and brand reputation. The findings indicate that the MSI GeForce GTX 1660 SUPER GAMING X offers the best value for performance, while the Gigabyte and PALIT models provide competitive pricing with higher discounts.

Introduction

This report documents the procurement process for NVIDIA GTX 1660 SUPER graphics cards intended for the company’s gaming and rendering departments. The scope covers price comparison, discount analysis, and recommendation for bulk purchase.

Methodology

  • Data Collection: Web scraping of Sigma Computer product pages using predefined search queries.
  • Data Extraction: Parsed product title, brand, current price, original price, discount percentage, and product URL.
  • Analysis: Calculated price per performance metric (price/discount) and visualized price distribution.
  • Recommendation: Ranked products by combined score of price, discount, and brand reputation.

Analysis

The MSI model, despite the lowest discount, offers the best price-to-performance ratio due to its lower base price. The Gigabyte and PALIT models provide higher discounts, which may be advantageous for bulk purchases where discount impact outweighs the higher absolute price. ZOTAC’s higher price and modest discount suggest it is less competitive in the current market.

Recommendations

  • Procure 50 units of the MSI GeForce GTX 1660 SUPER GAMING X for core rendering tasks.
  • Consider a mixed batch of 30 Gigabyte and 20 PALIT units for gaming rigs to leverage discount benefits.
  • Avoid ZOTAC model unless a specific feature set is required.
  • Negotiate bulk discount with Sigma Computer for all selected models.

Conclusion

The procurement strategy outlined balances cost efficiency with performance requirements. By selecting a combination of MSI, Gigabyte, and PALIT models, the company can achieve optimal budget allocation while meeting technical specifications.
