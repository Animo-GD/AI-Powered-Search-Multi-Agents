Executive Summary

This report reviews and compares six coffee / espresso machine options sourced from major Egyptian e‑retail channels (Amazon.eg, Ubuy, Jumia). The procurement process incorporated automated scraping, data harmonisation, price comparison, and budget‑impact assessment. The top‑ranked option (Nespresso Vertuo Plus by Magimix) offers the best overall value in terms of price, brand reputation, and feature set.

Introduction

Our objective is to identify the most cost‑effective, feature‑rich coffee machine that aligns with the café’s operational needs and brand positioning. By executing a structured procurement methodology and referencing trusted user reviews, the report furnishes actionable insights and a shortlist ready for procurement approval.

Methodology

  • Collection of search results from Amazon.eg, Ubuy, Jumia using pre‑defined queries.

  • Data normalization (price in Egyptian pound, brand extraction, discount calculation).
